 <p>PET VCI shrink film is a packaging material made from polyethylene terephthalate (PET) that incorporates volatile corrosion inhibitors (VCI). This film is specifically designed to provide robust physical protection and long-lasting corrosion resistance for metal products. When exposed to heat, the PET film shrinks tightly around the item, forming a secure, clear, and tough barrier against moisture, dust, and other environmental contaminants. The VCI compounds embedded in the film release vapor molecules that settle on the metal surface, creating a protective layer that prevents rust and corrosion. PET VCI shrink film is particularly useful in industries such as automotive, electronics, and machinery, where metal components require strong, transparent packaging that offers both visibility and protection. Its high tensile strength, resistance to tearing, and clarity make it ideal for applications where product visibility and durability are essential.</p>
