<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mono-oriented Polypropylene (MOPP) Packaging Film refers to a type of film made from polypropylene that is processed using a mono-orienting technique. This results in a film with enhanced strength, stiffness, and optical properties suitable for various packaging applications.</p>
