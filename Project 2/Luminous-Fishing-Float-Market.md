<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Luminous fishing floats are specialized fishing equipment that are equipped with a luminous material to enhance visibility in low-light conditions, making them ideal for night fishing activities. These floats assist anglers in tracking and detecting fish movements more efficiently.</p>
