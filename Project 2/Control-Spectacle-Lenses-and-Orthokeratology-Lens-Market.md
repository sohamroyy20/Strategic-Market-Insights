<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Myopia Control Spectacle Lenses and Orthokeratology Lens are advanced optical solutions designed to address myopia by modifying the way light enters the eye, thereby reducing the progression of nearsightedness. These lenses offer a non-invasive approach to managing myopia and have gained popularity for their effectiveness in controlling the condition.</p>
