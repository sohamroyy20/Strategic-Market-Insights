<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Open-ear headphones are a type of audio device designed to sit on the outer ear, allowing users to hear their surroundings while also listening to music or other audio content. Often used for outdoor activities or exercise, these headphones provide a more immersive experience compared to traditional closed-back headphones.</p>
