<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Multiwall paper bags are sustainable packaging solutions that find applications across various industries, including food, chemicals, agriculture, construction, and more. These bags are made of multiple layers of paper, enhancing their strength and durability. With growing environmental concerns, the demand for eco-friendly packaging solutions like multiwall paper bags is on the rise globally.</p>
