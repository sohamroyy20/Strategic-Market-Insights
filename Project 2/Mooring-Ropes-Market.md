<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mooring ropes are essential components used in the maritime industry to secure vessels to docks or other vessels, ensuring stability and safety during stationary periods. These ropes are designed to withstand various environmental conditions and loads, playing a crucial role in maritime operations and offshore activities.</p>
