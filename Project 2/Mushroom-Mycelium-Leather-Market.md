<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mushroom Mycelium Leather, also known as mycelium leather or fungal leather, is a sustainable and innovative alternative to traditional animal leather. It is derived from mycelium, the root structure of fungi, through a process that involves cultivation and processing to create a durable and eco-friendly material suitable for various applications.</p>
