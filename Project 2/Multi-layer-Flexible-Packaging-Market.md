<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Flexible packaging refers to a packaging material made of a variety of layers that provide barrier properties to protect the contents. The materials used can include plastics, films, and foils, among others. Multi-layer flexible packaging involves the combination of different layers to enhance protection and functionality.</p>
