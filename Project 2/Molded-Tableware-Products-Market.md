<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Molded tableware products refer to a range of tableware items such as plates, bowls, cups, and cutlery that are manufactured using molding processes. These products are typically made from materials like plastic, bamboo, or sugarcane, and are widely used in households, restaurants, and other dining establishments.</p>
