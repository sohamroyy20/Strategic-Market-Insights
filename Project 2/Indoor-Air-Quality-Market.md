<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Indoor air quality (IAQ) refers to the air quality within and around buildings and structures, especially as it relates to the health and comfort of the occupants. It encompasses factors such as air purity, temperature, and humidity levels. Ensuring optimal indoor air quality is essential for maintaining a healthy and productive indoor environment.<p>

<h2>Market Dynamics:</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Increasing awareness regarding the importance of indoor air quality for health and well-being is driving market growth. Consumers are more proactive in adopting air quality solutions to mitigate risks associated with indoor pollutants.</li>
  <li>Stringent government regulations and guidelines related to indoor air quality control are pushing industries and households to invest in advanced air purification and filtration systems.</li>
  <li>The rapid urbanization and industrialization across developing economies are amplifying concerns about indoor air pollution, creating a significant demand for IAQ solutions.</li>
  <li>Technological advancements in air quality monitoring devices and systems are enhancing the capabilities of detecting and addressing indoor air pollutants effectively.</li>
</ul>
