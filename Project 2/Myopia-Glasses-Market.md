<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Myopia glasses, also known as nearsighted glasses, are corrective lenses specifically designed to improve vision for individuals with myopia. These glasses help in focusing distant objects clearly by correcting the refractive error in the eye's lens. With the rising prevalence of myopia globally, the demand for myopia glasses has seen substantial growth in recent years.</p>
