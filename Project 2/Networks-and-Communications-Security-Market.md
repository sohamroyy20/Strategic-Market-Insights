<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>The Networks and Communications Security market encompasses various technologies and solutions aimed at securing communication networks from cyber threats, unauthorized access, and data breaches. This market includes hardware, software, and services designed to protect sensitive information and ensure the integrity and confidentiality of network communications.</p>
