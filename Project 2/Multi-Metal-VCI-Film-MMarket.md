<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Multi Metal VCI (Volatility Corrosion Inhibitor) Film is a specialized packaging material used in the metalworking industry to protect metal components and machinery from corrosion during storage and transportation. This film releases chemical compounds that create a protective atmosphere around the metal, preventing rust and corrosion.</p>
