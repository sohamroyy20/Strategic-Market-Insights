<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Tool Tethering Kits are essential safety accessories used in various industries to prevent dropped tools and equipment. These kits consist of tethers, lanyards, anchors, and other securing mechanisms designed to keep tools tethered to a worker or a designated location during use.</p>

    <li>Rising awareness regarding the potential hazards of dropped objects in industrial settings is prompting industries like construction, oil and gas, and manufacturing to invest in reliable tethering solutions to mitigate risks and enhance workplace safety.</li>
