 <p>Aseptic packaging combines aseptic filling technology, and has a series of advantages such as long storage time, no need to add preservatives, effective barrier against phosgene and microbial invasion, avoidance of loss of nutrients and flavor, and volume specification. It is widely used in packaging containers for liquid milk, non carbonated soft drinks and other foods. At present, the commonly used packaging of liquid milk in the market mainly includes glass bottles, plastic bags, Baili bags, sterile packaging, etc. Compared with traditional packaging containers, sterile packaging has the characteristics of no need for refrigeration, long shelf life, shading, heat insulation, sterility, low cost, convenient transportation, etc. It is significantly superior to the traditional packaging in functional characteristics according to the molding appearance. The sterile packaging is divided into pillow bags, brick bags and diamond bags. The pillow bags can be stored for about 45-60 days, Brick bags and diamond bags are stored for 180 days or more.</p>
