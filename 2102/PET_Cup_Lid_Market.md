 <p>A PET cup lid is a type of disposable lid made from polyethylene terephthalate (PET) plastic. It is designed to fit securely on cups, commonly used for beverages such as coffee, tea, and soft drinks. PET cup lids are clear, durable, and resistant to cracking, providing a reliable and convenient way to prevent spills and maintain the temperature of the drink. Additionally, PET is recyclable, making these lids a more environmentally friendly choice compared to some other plastics.</p>
 
 
 
 <p><strong>Material Type</strong>: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) Cup Lids are widely used in the food and beverage industry for covering disposable cups, offering convenience and hygiene benefits.</p>
