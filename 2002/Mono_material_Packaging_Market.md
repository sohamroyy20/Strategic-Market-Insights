 <p>Mono-material packaging refers to packaging that is made from a single type of material, such as plastic, paper, or metal, rather than a combination of different materials. The primary goal of mono-material packaging is to make the recycling process more efficient by simplifying the sorting and separation of materials. Mono-material packaging is an important trend in the packaging industry as companies seek to improve the sustainability of their products and reduce their environmental impact.</p>
