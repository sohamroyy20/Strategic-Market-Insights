 <p>Microneedle skincare patches, also known as dermal or transdermal patches, are a type of advanced skincare product that utilizes microneedle technology to deliver active ingredients directly into the skin. Unlike traditional skincare products that rely on the skin&#39;s permeability for absorption, these patches have tiny, microscopic needles that can penetrate the skin&#39;s outer barrier, the stratum corneum, without causing pain or damage. Microneedle skincare patches are often used for a variety of purposes such as anti-aging treatments, improving skin hydration, reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, treating acne, and even for hair growth when applied to the scalp.</p>
