Some temperature control cups come with built-in electronic temperature control systems, allowing users to set and regulate the temperature via digital displays or smartphone apps. Commonly used for coffee, tea, or other beverages, these cups provide convenience for users by ensuring drinks stay at an ideal temperature, improving overall drinking experience.</p>
